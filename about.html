About

I'm a Math and Statistics graduate from McMaster University (Class of 2025) with a focus on data analytics and modelling. I'm currently working as a Research Data Analyst at the National Institute for Materials Science in Tsukuba, Japan.

Before that I worked as an Inventory Data Analyst at Advanced Skytech Canada in Markham, Ontario, where I grew up.

Outside of work I spend a lot of time outdoors — I've been fishing all over Ontario since I was young, from biking to local creeks to driving hours up north. I also enjoy sewing and mending clothes, particularly using sashiko, which I taught myself.
