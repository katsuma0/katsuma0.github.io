Data Projects

Projects built using things I've learned in school and taught myself along the way.

Used Car Price Prediction Model

March 2025  ·  R, RStudio

Built a linear regression model on 100,000+ Craigslist listings to predict used car prices. Achieved an R² of 0.691 and MAE of ~$3,781. Validated against real listings from Autotrader, achieving 11–13% error on common vehicles.

Carbon Cycle & Sustainable Deforestation Model

January 2025  ·  R, RStudio

Dynamic systems model analyzing deforestation impact on carbon storage across an ecosystem with seasonal climate variations. Presented findings to a lecture hall.

DSA Cryptosystem

December 2024  ·  Python

Hybrid cryptosystem combining the Chinese Remainder Theorem and RSA principles. Demonstrated system vulnerabilities through cryptanalysis of public key exposure.
