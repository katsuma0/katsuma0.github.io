About

I graduated from McMaster University in 2025 with a degree in Mathematics and Statistics. I currently work as a Research Data Analyst at the National Institute for Materials Science in Tsukuba, Japan, where I build data templates for research teams. Before that I worked as an Inventory Data Analyst at a computer refurbishing company in Markham, tracking hard drive stock and building dashboards.

Outside of work I fish whenever I can and sew and mend my own clothes, mostly using sashiko, which I taught myself.
