Wallet #1

Nov 2024

My first attempt at making something from scratch. A bifold wallet sewn together from 3 pieces of fabric. The fabric I chose was too loud and the dimensions came out odd so it never got any real use. Aligning the pieces in a way that made it actually functional was harder than I expected.

Jeans Knee Repair With Sashiko

Nov 2024

My favourite pair of thrifted jeans worn out with a giant hole in the knee. I backed it with a contrasting black fabric and switched a plus sign pattern over it in sashiko.

Looking back, I spaced the plus signs too far apart because I rushed it.

Experimental Hemming

Oct 2024

Hemmed multiple pairs of jeans and used it as an excuse to experiment with stitching patterns.

Getting Started

Sept 2024

I wanted to shorten the cuffs on a sweater and decided to just try sewing it myself. Around the same time I started experimenting with stitching patterns onto scrap fabric to get a feel for it.
